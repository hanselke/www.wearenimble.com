Process

We unearth hidden truths and weave your story.

Discovery & Research

We start with several days of meetings and interviews with key stakeholders/decision makers as well as market research, including focus groups, to understand and establish the communication needs at hand and set measurable goals.

Develop Messaging

Once the foundation of the brand has been articulated and a strategy & process de ned, we begin working to visually and verbally express the brand in whatever media has been designated. Initial design concepts and major marketing messages are presented.

Express the Brand

Following our strategies, we finalize the design of all touchpoints and various other brand assets as we begin to express the brand across appropriate media channels.
